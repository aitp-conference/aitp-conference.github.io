Q: Can I get a public bus to Aussois?

A: Yes, you can take the public "Altibus" line from Modane, and get off at the Aussois Office de Tourisme stop, which is close to the Paul Langevin center. You need to book in advance, but it’s cheap (8€ per person), and conveniently the summer service runs until September 5. See https://www.altibus.com/ .

Q: When is the last session?

A: The last session is on Friday morning and it ends with the lunch.

Q: When does the center close?

A: The center usually closes after lunch on Friday. They are relaxed about it and people can stay e.g. until 4pm if they want.

Q: What about the return trip? 

A: People just enter their departure times and share taxis. The center will be collecting this information during the conference and optimizing the taxi size/sharing. You can also tell us in the registration form. 

Q: Any more info about the public bus (altibus.com)?

A: On Sunday it leaves from Modane to Aussois at 17:00 or 14:00.  On Friday it leaves from Aussois to Modane at 11:52 or 14:42. 

Q: Any more info about travel to Modane?

A: Apart from travelling via Paris/Lyon and Milan/Turin, consider also travelling via Switzerland (Geneva, Zurich, etc.). There are trains available from Geneva to Chambery and from there to Modane.

Q: My plane/train was delayed and I will not reach Modane in time to catch the 19:10 bus. What should I do?

A: Take a taxi from Modane to Aussois.
