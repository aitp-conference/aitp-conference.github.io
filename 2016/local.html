1st Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Theorem Proving

AITP 2016

April 3–7, 2016, Obergurgl, Austria

Local Information

The conference will start in the afternoon of the 3rd. We will provide a bus from Innsbruck to Obergurgl at 11am on the 3rd.

If you want to join the bus, you can either arrive to Innsbruck in the morning of the 3rd or arrive earlier and book a hotel in Innsbruck for the night 2nd/3rd. There are many reasonably priced hotel options in Innsbruck. Depending on your arrival near the city center (train station) or near the airport may be convenient.

Or you can decide to travel directly to Obergurgl yourself. Then you can take a train to Ötztal-Bahnhof and take a public bus to Obergurgl from there. This is particularily useful if you come from the Switzerland direction, then you do not need to pass by Innsbruck.

Remember to take clothes appropriate for the weather, in particular if you like to join skiing (ski-lift passes 50m from the conference center) you may bring with you some equipment. Rental is also possible in the Obergurgl village.
